About ViLo Worksheets

Free printable worksheets for kids in India — Pre-KG to Class 5. Includes Math Master (rapid mental math drills for Class 1-5), Maze worksheets, Sudoku for kids, coloring pages, letter tracing, number tracing, addition, multiplication, shape recognition, dot-to-dot, and 20+ more activity types. Built and maintained by a solo developer in India. No sign-up, no watermark on the PDF, no email collection. Use freely at home, in school, or in your coaching class.

How to use

  1. Pick a levelPre-KG, KG, Class 1-5, Coloring, Maze, Sudoku, or Math Master. Each is tuned to age-appropriate activities.
  2. Set page count and GenerateChoose 5 to 15 pages with the slider, then click Generate — your worksheet appears in seconds. Don't like the mix? Click "New" at the top of the worksheet for a fresh version with the same level.
  3. Print or shareClick PDF for an instant print-ready file, or click Share to send the link to another parent or teacher.

Frequently asked questions

Is this really free? Forever?

Yes. No paywall, no upgrade tier, no premium-only worksheets. Every activity, every grade, every theme is free. The site is built and run by a solo developer in India on a hobby budget.

Do these worksheets cover CBSE or state board topics?

They cover the foundational reading, writing, counting, and arithmetic skills taught across CBSE, ICSE, and Indian state boards for pre-primary and primary classes. We don't claim formal curriculum mapping — we focus on the universal skills that show up across all boards.

Can I print these for my classroom or coaching class?

Yes — free for any educational use including schools, coaching classes, tuition centres, anganwadis, and daycares. No attribution required, though it's appreciated.

Are these worksheets available in Hindi?

The interface has a Hindi (हिंदी) toggle for menu labels and prompts. Worksheet content (letters, numbers, words) is currently in English. Hindi worksheet content is on the roadmap.

What ages are these for?

Pre-KG = ages 3-4, KG = 5-6, Class 1 = 6-7, Class 2 = 7-8, Class 3 = 8-9, Class 4 = 9-10, Class 5 = 10-11. Colouring and maze worksheets work across the full age range.

Will the same URL show the same worksheet to my friend?

Yes. Every share URL has a unique seed that reproduces the exact same worksheet. Send the link via WhatsApp or email and your friend sees the same sheet on their device.

Do you have a mobile app?

ViLo Apps for Android is in development. The mobile app will mirror the worksheet generator with offline-first PDF download. No firm release date yet — see the Apps tab on the homepage for updates.

What is Math Master?

Math Master is our rapid mental math drill activity for Class 1-5. Each sheet has dozens of quick math problems (addition, subtraction, multiplication based on the class level) designed to build calculation speed and accuracy. Print, time your child, and watch their mental math improve over weeks of practice.

How is this different from worksheetfun or k5learning?

India-flavoured themes (autorickshaws, alphonso mangos, Indian transport), generated fresh each click instead of a fixed library, free without any sign-up, and built by a single developer paying close attention to feedback. The international sites are great too — we just fill a slightly different niche.
