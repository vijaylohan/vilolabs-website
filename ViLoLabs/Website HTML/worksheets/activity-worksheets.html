A whole mix, not the same page twice

A stack of twenty identical addition sheets is how you get a bored child. These packs go the other way — open one and you'll find tracing on one page, a matching puzzle on the next, then some counting, a pattern to finish, a few gentle sums. The variety is the point; it keeps a young child moving through the pack instead of stalling on page two.

And because every pack is generated fresh, you're never handing over the same worksheet again. A nursery child can do a new pack each morning; a Class 3 child can keep a different one in their bag every day of the week.

Pitched to the right class

What lands in the pack changes with the class you pick. A nursery or pre-KG pack leans on pictures — counting animals, tracing simple lines, matching pairs. Kindergarten adds early letters and numbers. By Class 1 to 5 the activities get properly challenging, with bigger numbers, word and letter puzzles and trickier logic. You're never handing a five-year-old a ten-year-old's sheet, or the other way round.

Free, and yours to print

Every activity worksheet here is free — no account, no watermark, nothing to buy. Print a pack straight away or save it as a PDF for later, and run off as many as your printer can take. For the price of a little ink you've got an endless supply of things to keep a child busy and learning.

Common questions

What's inside one activity worksheet pack?

Each pack mixes around twenty different activity types — things like letter and number tracing, matching, counting, patterns, odd-one-out, dot-to-dot, simple sums and puzzles. You get a variety in every pack rather than page after page of the same exercise.

Which classes are covered?

From nursery and pre-KG through kindergarten and up to Class 5. The activities and difficulty are pitched to each level, so a nursery pack is gentle picture work while a Class 5 pack is more demanding.

Do I get the same worksheet each time?

No. Every time you generate, the maker builds a brand-new pack with a fresh mix of activities, so a child can do a new set every day without repeats.

Is it free to print or save as a PDF?

Completely free — no sign-up, no watermark. You can print the pack directly or save it as a PDF, and make as many as you like.
