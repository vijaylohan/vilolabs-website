    Good to Know

    Common questions.

    How many PDFs can I merge?

    As many as your browser memory can hold — typically dozens of normal-size PDFs without trouble.

    Is this free and watermark-free?

    Yes. No sign up, no watermark, and the merged PDF is exactly your originals stitched together.

    Are my files uploaded?

    No. The merge happens entirely in your browser using the open-source pdf-lib library. Files never leave your device.

    Will bookmarks or links survive?

    Pages, text and embedded images are preserved exactly. Some PDF metadata and bookmarks may not carry over from every PDF.
