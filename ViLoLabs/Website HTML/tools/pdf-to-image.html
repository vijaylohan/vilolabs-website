Good to Know

Common questions.

What formats can I export?

JPG and PNG. JPG is smaller and best for photos or scans. PNG is lossless and best for screenshots, diagrams, or anything with sharp text.

What resolution will I get?

Pick between Standard (96 DPI), High (192 DPI) and Ultra (288 DPI). Higher DPI means larger files but sharper text and detail when printed or zoomed.

Will my PDF be uploaded?

No. pdf.js reads your file directly in your browser — nothing leaves your device.

Can I download all images at once?

Yes — click Download All as ZIP and we package every page into a single ZIP file. One click, one file, then extract on your device.
