Good to Know

Common questions.

Why a 200 KB preset?

Many Indian government forms (UPSC, SSC, IBPS, state boards, exam portals) require a passport photo or signature image under 200 KB. The preset hits that target automatically.

How does target KB mode work?

We keep your original dimensions and reduce JPG quality step by step until the file fits. If it still can't fit at low quality, we gently downscale dimensions until it does.

Does this keep transparency?

PNG keeps transparency. JPG does not — we fill transparent areas with white. Choose your format in the Format dropdown.

Are my images uploaded?

No. Everything happens in your browser — your image never leaves your device.
